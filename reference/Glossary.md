# Glossary — Islamic Will Terms (Arabic · English · Legal)

A bilingual reference for users and the AI agent. Terms are organized by category.

---

## Part 1 — Islamic Inheritance Terms (*Ilm al-Fara'id*)

| Arabic Term | Transliteration | English Meaning | Notes |
|-------------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| الوصية | *Al-Wasiyya* | Islamic Will / Testament | The document directing estate distribution |
| الفرائض | *Al-Fara'id* | Science of Islamic Inheritance | The Quranic system of fixed inheritance shares |
| الميراث | *Al-Mirath* | Inheritance | The actual property inherited |
| المُوَرِّث | *Al-Muwarith* | Deceased / Testator | The person who has died and left property |
| الوارث | *Al-Warith* | Heir | Person who inherits |
| التَّرِكَة | *Al-Tarikah* | The Estate | All property left by the deceased |

|-------------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| التجهيز والتكفين | *Tajheez wa Takfeen* | Funeral & Burial Preparation | First priority — must be paid before distribution |
| الغُسل | *Ghusl* | Ritual Washing of the Body | Performed by same-gender Muslim |

## Part 3 — Categories of Heirs

| Arabic Term | Transliteration | English Meaning | Notes |
|-------------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| أصحاب الفروض | *Ashab al-Furud* | Quranic Sharers | Those with fixed fractional shares in the Quran |
| ذوو الفروض | *Zawil Furud* | Heirs with Fixed Shares | Same as Ashab al-Furud |
| العَصَبَة | *Al-Asabah* (sing. *Asaba*) | Residuaries | Heirs who take whatever remains after fixed shares |
| عَصَبة بالنفس | *Asaba bi-Nafs* | Residuary in Own Right | All male agnates (son, father, brother, uncle) |
| عَصَبة بالغير | *Asaba bi-Ghayr* | Residuary by Another | Female heir who inherits residue alongside male counterpart |
| عَصَبة مع الغير | *Asaba Ma'a al-Ghayr* | Residuary with Another | Full/paternal sisters become Asaba with daughters |
| ذوو الأرحام | *Dhawu al-Arham* | Distant Kindred | Non-Sharer, non-Asaba relatives who inherit only if no closer heirs exist |

## Part 5 — Inheritance Mechanics

| Arabic Term | Transliteration | English Meaning | Notes |
|-------------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| العَوْل | *Al-Awl* (also *Al-Aul*) | Pro-Rata Reduction | Applied when fixed shares total > 100%; all shares reduced proportionally |
| الرَّدّ | *Al-Radd* | Return / Reversion | Surplus returned to non-spouse heirs when fixed shares < 100% and no Asaba exist |
| الحَجْب | *Al-Hajb* | Exclusion / Blocking | Rule by which a closer heir blocks a more distant heir |
| حجب حرمان | *Hajb Hirman* | Total Exclusion | Heir is completely blocked from inheriting |
| حجب نقصان | *Hajb Nuqsan* | Partial Exclusion | Heir's share is reduced (not eliminated) by presence of another heir |
| المُقَاسَمَة | *Al-Muqasama* | Sharing / Division | Shafi'i method of distributing residue between Grandfather and Brothers |
| المُشْتَرَكَة | *Al-Mushtaraka* | "The Shared" Case | Specific scenario: Husband + Mother + Uterine Brothers + Full Brother |
| النَّصِيب | *Al-Naseeb* | Share / Portion | The specific portion of the estate an heir receives |
| العَصَبِيَّة | *Al-Asabiyya* | Agnatic Relationship | Relationship through males only |
| النَّسَب | *Al-Nasab* | Lineage / Bloodline | Legal blood relationship establishing inheritance rights |
| الإِرث | *Al-Irth* | Inheritance (general) | General term for the inheritance |

## Part 6 — Impediments

| Arabic Term | Transliteration | English Meaning | Notes |
|-------------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| موانع الإرث | *Mawani' al-Mirath* | Impediments to Inheritance | Conditions that bar an heir from receiving their share |
| القَتْل | *Al-Qatl* | Homicide | Killer cannot inherit from the victim |
| اختلاف الدين | *Ikhtilaf al-Din* | Difference of Religion | Non-Muslim cannot inherit from Muslim via Mirath |
| التبني | *Al-Tabanni* | Legal Adoption | Not recognized in Islamic law as establishing bloodline (Nasab) |
| المفقود | *Al-Mafqud* | Missing Person | Treated as alive; share reserved until legal death declaration |
| الحَمْل | *Al-Haml* | Unborn Fetus | Has inheritance rights if born alive |
| الغَرَق | *Al-Gharaq* | Drowning (simultaneous death) | Neither person inherits from the other in simultaneous death |

## Part 8 — Tennessee Legal Terms

| Legal Term | Meaning | Tennessee Statute |
|------------|---------|------------------|
| **Testator** | Person who makes a will | T.C.A. § 32-1-101 |
| **Executor / Personal Representative** | Person who administers the estate | T.C.A. § 35-15-816 |
| **Probate** | Court process to validate a will and oversee estate distribution | T.C.A. § 30-1-101 |
| **Intestacy** | Dying without a valid will; state law dictates distribution | T.C.A. § 31-2-104 |
| **Elective Share** | Surviving spouse's right to claim a percentage of the estate against the will | T.C.A. § 31-4-101 |
| **Waiver of Elective Share** | Spouse's voluntary agreement to accept the will's distribution instead of the statutory share | T.C.A. § 31-4-102 |
| **Self-Proving Affidavit** | Notarized statement from testator and witnesses at signing; eliminates need for witnesses to testify at probate | T.C.A. § 32-1-104 |
| **Disinterested Witness** | Witness who is not a beneficiary under the will | T.C.A. § 32-1-104 |
| **Holographic Will** | Handwritten will (no witnesses required, but harder to probate) | T.C.A. § 32-1-105 |
| **Codicil** | Amendment to an existing will (must be executed with the same formality as the will) | Common law |
| **Beneficiary** | Person who receives a gift under a will or beneficiary designation | — |
| **Bequest** | Gift of personal property under a will | — |
| **Devise** | Gift of real property under a will | — |
| **Residuary Estate** | What remains after specific gifts and debts are paid | — |
| **Per Stirpes** | Distribution "by branch" — descendants of a deceased heir take that heir's share | — |
| **In Terrorem Clause** | No-contest clause — challenger forfeits their share | T.C.A. § 32-3-119 |
| **Guardianship** | Court-supervised authority over a minor child | T.C.A. § 36-6-106 |
| **Joint Tenancy with Right of Survivorship** | Co-ownership where surviving owner inherits automatically | Property law |
| **Tenants in Common** | Co-ownership where each share passes through the owner's will | Property law |
| **POD (Payable on Death)** | Bank account beneficiary designation | — |
| **TOD (Transfer on Death)** | Investment account or vehicle beneficiary designation | — |
| **RUFADAA** | Revised Uniform Fiduciary Access to Digital Assets Act — governs Executor's access to digital accounts | T.C.A. § 35-8 |
